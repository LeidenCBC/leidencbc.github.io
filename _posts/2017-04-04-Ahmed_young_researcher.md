The selection committee of the [BioSB Young Investigator Award 2017](https://www.aanmelder.nl/biosb2017/wiki/218447/biosb%20young%20investigator%20award%202017#.WS_6c2iGM54) has selected CBC faculty member Ahmed Mahfouz as this year's award winner! Ahmed will receive the prize of &euro; 500 at the annual Dutch Bioinformatics & Systems Biology conference. The award ceremony and the honorary lecture will take place on April 4 at the congress center *De Wereld* in Lunteren.
